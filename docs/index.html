About Me

Hello~ My name is Ryan, I'm a life long learner based in Ohio, USA.

I've spent the last decade working in corporate america as a .NET and SQL developer supporting a commercial trading, risk and accounting system. During that time I have been full time developer, project manager, change managerment coordinator, quality assurance lead, etc.

I enjoy volunteering my time with various causes supporting Diversity, Equity and Inclusion. In particular I am heavily involved with Spectrum of Findlay, an LGBTQ+ community advocacy group, as well as International Friendship Center of Logan County where I volunteer taking junior and high school students to Japan on a sister city program to Suzuka.

The rest of the time I enjoy being at home with my dog and cat. I am always picking up a new hobby. Some of my hobbies include: baking, knitting, quilting, frementing foods, cooking fancy foods and most recently got a Cricut machine and am starting some new things there.
